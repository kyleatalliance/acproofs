A Simpler Path to Retirement income 

For many DC savers, the transition into retirement can be fraught with complexity. Faced with a wide and often overwhelming range of decumulation products, members are expected to make high-stakes decisions that will shape their financial security for the rest of their lives – and many do this without the support or confidence they need.

Managed Income for Life is a new solution that addresses this challenge head-on, enabling members to move seamlessly into drawdown without switching to a separate provider or making difficult choices. Brought to life through the combined expertise of Phoenix CIS, TPT Retirement Solutions and AllianceBernstein, the solution allows key retirement risks – such as longevity, investment volatility, inflation, and sustainability – to be managed on members’ behalf. The result is a simpler, more secure retirement journey where members can feel confident about their income and stay in control of their future.
